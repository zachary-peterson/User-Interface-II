Three months later, I hadn't left New Zealand and becasue of a soon to expire passport, couldn't apply for a work visa.


But, it all worked out, because I know now where my future forever home will be.


I decided to get back in to coding because it's a skill set that can be used anywhere, and I hope to eventually find something sustainable there!
